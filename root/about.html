•  About Us

Sushi Systems is a technology company founded in 2024 by brothers Mustafa and Mahmut Yavuz Garip, with the goal of creating a virtual universe modeled on the laws of nature.

Our mission is to create virtual environments built on the principles of natural sciences, designed to resemble and function like the real world, using artificial intelligence and simulation technology. We aim to make these environments come to life with accurate and fast real-time simulations that can be applied not only in games but also across other fields.

Many tech companies follow the “move fast and break things” doctrine, putting speed above quality. Sushi Systems takes the opposite path. We don’t chase trends or push out rushed projects, we focus on building with care and precision.

Our goal is to draw from the natural sciences to create virtual worlds that mirror reality, combining artificial intelligence and simulation to leave behind works that bring value both to computers and to people.

We aim to create experiences that are meaningful, lasting, and built with care, blending our heart, mind and realism into everything we make.
