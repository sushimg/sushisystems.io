Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 11, 2025

Thank you for playing Project: mobileRTS. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. No Data Collection

Project: mobileRTS does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. The game operates fully offline and does not use analytics, advertising, tracking tools, or any third-party SDKs.

2. Permissions

The game does not request or require access to your device data such as contacts, camera, or location. The only permission that might be requested is for Storage access. This permission is used solely to save your game progress on your device and for no other purpose. We do not access, read, or modify any of your personal files.

3. Third-Party Services

The game does not use any third-party services (e.g., Google Play Games, analytics, ads, or social plugins) that collect data.

4. Children’s Privacy

This game is designed to be suitable for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected whatsoever, we are fully compliant with children's privacy regulations such as the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5. Updates to This Policy

If future updates introduce online features, advertising, or analytics, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly, and users will be notified through the Play Store page.
